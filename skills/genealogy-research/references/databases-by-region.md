| Service | URL | What it contains | Access |
|---------|-----|-----------------|--------|
| Geneteka | geneteka.genealodzy.pl | 47M+ indexes of parish registers. **Use "pobliskie parafie (15km)" checkbox** | Free |
| Skanoteka | skanoteka.genealodzy.pl | Original scans of parish registers | Free |
| Szukaj w Archiwach | szukajwarchiwach.gov.pl | Scans from Polish state archives | Free |
| Geneszukacz | geneszukacz.genealodzy.pl | Meta-search across all PTG databases | Free |
| Metryki.genealodzy.pl | metryki.genealodzy.pl | Indexed vital records with source links | Free |
| Poznan Project | poznan-project.psnc.pl | Marriages from Greater Poland region | Free |
| Baza PRADZIAD | szukajwarchiwach.gov.pl/pradziad | Directory of surviving parish books (what exists where) | Free |
| Archiwum Diecezjalne | varies by diocese | Diocesan archives hold pre-1900 church books | Visit/mail |

**Tips for Poland:**
- Parish books before 1826: Latin. After 1826: Russian (Russian partition), German (Prussian), Polish (Austrian/Galicia)
- Civil registration: 1808 (Duchy of Warsaw), continued in Russian partition. Austrian partition: church books served as civil records
- Geneteka indexing is incomplete — check date coverage per parish
- FamilySearch has microfilms of many Polish parishes — check catalog by place name

## Russia / USSR

**Military (WWII) — free, no login, browser-friendly:**

| Service | URL | What it contains |
|---------|-----|-----------------|
| Pamyat Naroda | pamyat-naroda.ru | Awards + award sheets (описание подвига), combat path, loss reports, officer cards. Richest single source; each person = a `hero-<id>` profile with document tabs |
| OBD Memorial | obd-memorial.ru | WWII irrecoverable losses (killed / missing) — loss reports often name the next of kin |
| Podvig Naroda | podvignaroda.ru | Feats and award orders |

**Repression / other index bases:**

| Service | URL | What it contains |
|---------|-----|-----------------|
| Open List | ru.openlist.wiki | Victims of political repression (largest open DB); use to confirm/deny dekulakization |
| Bessmertny Barak | bessmertnybarak.ru | Repression, camps |
| VGD | vgd.ru | Forum + volunteer lookups; regional expertise |

**Archives — digitized registers (the path to vital/parish records):**

| Service | URL | What it contains | Access |
|---------|-----|-----------------|--------|
| ГИС УИАД / online.archives.ru | online.archives.ru | Unified portal linking many regional archives' digitized files; virtual reading room | Registration via national e-ID; may use a departmental certificate → **navigator mode** |
| Regional archive portals (АИС) | vary by region (e.g. `af.<region>-archives.ru`, `<code>.archives.ru`) | Digitized fondy/opisi/dela incl. metrical books | Free browse of catalog; images may need login |
| RGIA | fgurgia.ru | Russian Historical Archive (pre-1917), federal | Visit/mail; some catalog online |
| GARF | statearchive.ru | State Archive of the RF | Visit/mail |
| Yandex/FamilySearch catalog | familysearch.org | Microfilms of many Russian Empire parishes — search by place | Free (FS) |

**Tips for Russia / former Russian Empire:**
- **Record types:** parish registers (метрические книги — births/marriages/deaths,
  each entry names both parents → the key source for maiden names); revision
  lists (ревизские сказки, censuses 1719–1858); confessional lists (исповедные
  ведомости, annual parish census — excellent for family reconstruction).
- **Where they live:** by the region *of the time*, in that region's state
  archive. Boundaries shifted — a place's modern region may differ from its
  historical uyezd/gubernia, which changes *which archive* holds the book.
- **Archive addressing:** documents are cited as **fond / opis / delo**
  (фонд / опись / дело) + list/frame. Record these exactly in citations.
- **Index ≠ content (critical):** most archive search engines index only the
  *titles* of files (fond/opis/delo) and personalia in some fonds — **not** the
  peasant names inside metrical books. A surname "not found" in the portal
  usually means the relevant book must be read page-by-page, not that the family
  is absent. Identify the right parish's book, then browse it by eye.
- **Access quirks:** state systems often use self-signed/departmental
  certificates and login via national e-ID (Gosuslugi) tied to a person → the
  agent can't authenticate. Use **navigator mode** (user opens the site in the
  agent's browser; agent then navigates and reads).
- **Calendar:** Julian until February 1918 — add 12–13 days for the Gregorian date.

## DNA and Genetic Genealogy

| Service | URL | What it offers |
|---------|-----|---------------|
| AncestryDNA | ancestry.com/dna | Largest database, ethnicity + matches |
| 23andMe | 23andme.com | Health + ancestry, smaller match DB |
| MyHeritage DNA | myheritage.com/dna | Good European coverage |
| FamilyTreeDNA | familytreedna.com | Y-DNA, mtDNA, autosomal |
| GEDmatch | gedmatch.com | Free comparison tool (upload from any service) |
| DNAPainter | dnapainter.com | Chromosome mapping tool |

**Tips:**
- Start with AncestryDNA (largest database) or MyHeritage (best for European roots)
- Upload raw DNA to GEDmatch for cross-platform matching
- Y-DNA traces paternal line; mtDNA traces maternal line; autosomal covers all ancestors (fades after ~6 generations)
